Description

Libre is a page in Facebook created as a social media experiment

In 2016, I decided to create a Facebook Page where I could prove that good quality content and a relevant message can provide organic engagement and reach. 

TagsContentDigital MarketingDate12-20-2020














What did happen.

Topics like good vibes, self-growth and awareness were important in 2016, so I use that theme to develop content that could build a community through engagement.

After a few months and less than 100 CAD to push some of the post, I was able to earn +90K followers and content that reach more than 8 million views.
